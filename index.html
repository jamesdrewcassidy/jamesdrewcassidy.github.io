Tooling & Automation

One of my strongest assets is identifying operational bottlenecks and building custom software solutions to fix them. These are tools I designed and deployed at Omni Technologies.

Digital Device Checklist

The previous inspection process relied on paper reports which were prone to data entry errors. I built a digital interface that field inspectors use on-site.

Impact
  • • Eliminated paper-to-digital transcription errors.
  • • Standardized data collection across the team.

PDF Bookmark Automation

Reviewing massive inspection reports was slow due to lack of navigation. I scripted a tool that automatically generates bookmarks for every device and section in the PDF.

Impact
  • • Drastically reduced report review time.
  • • Improved navigation for clients and AHJs.

Report Parser & Emailer

Manually typing up discrepancy emails was repetitive. I built a parser that reads the report, extracts failed devices, and generates the client email automatically.

Impact
  • • Ensured consistent, professional communication.
  • • Freeing up hours of administrative time per week.
